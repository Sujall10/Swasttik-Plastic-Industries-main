Industry Applications

Swasttik Plastic Industries offers contract moulding services across multiple industries, supporting OEMs and manufacturers with cost-effective and scalable plastic component production.

Electric Applications

Switch & Socket Covers – High-quality, heat-resistant plastic for smooth operation and safety.

Cable Management Components – Includes cable ties, clips, conduits, and holders for organized wiring.

Electrical Enclosures – Protective casings for electrical circuits and boards, available in various sizes.

Plug & Adapter Casings – Robust plastic bodies for power plugs, chargers, and adapters.

Insulating Parts – Custom-moulded components used for insulation and protection in electrical systems.

Food application

Airtight Containers – Ideal for preserving food freshness and preventing leakage.

Food Storage Boxes – Microwave-safe and freezer-friendly options for everyday use.

Water Bottles – Ergonomic, BPA-free bottles suitable for home and travel.

Serving Trays & Bowls – Stylish and practical for both home and catering use.

Packaging Components – Custom-moulded parts for food packaging applications.

Household applications

Storage Containers – Sturdy and stackable for organizing homes efficiently.

Buckets and Tubs – Available in various sizes, designed for heavy-duty usage.

Kitchenware & Organizers – Includes trays, racks, and containers to keep kitchens neat.

Bathroom Accessories – Soap dishes, toothbrush holders, and utility stands.

Multi-Purpose Utility Items – Lightweight yet strong products made for convenience.

Healthcare application

Syringe & IV Set Parts – Plungers, barrels, and caps for disposable medical use.

Diagnostic Device Housings – Casings for tools like thermometers and glucometers.

Surgical Tool Components – Handles and grips for surgical instruments.

Medical Consumables – Items like urine containers, test tube racks, and sample cups.

Insert Moulded Parts – Plastic parts with metal or electronic inserts for advanced applications
