About Us

Swasttik Plastic Industries is a professionally managed manufacturer specializing in contract molding and engineering plastics solutions for electrical and industrial applications. With a strong monthly production capacity of 10 tons, we support businesses seeking reliable, scalable, and cost-efficient plastic manufacturing.

Our product portfolio includes trefoil clamps, cable ties, and cable spacers, developed to meet stringent performance, safety, and durability requirements. Through optimized manufacturing processes and careful material selection, we help our customers address challenges such as capacity limitations, production cost pressures, and the need for consistent quality.

At Swasttik Plastic Industries, we place strong emphasis on engineering precision, process control, and long-term partnerships. By working closely with our clients, we deliver flexible manufacturing support and dependable solutions that enhance operational efficiency and product reliability.

Our approach is driven by quality, transparency, and continuous improvement, positioning us as a trusted partner for companies looking to strengthen their supply chain with dependable plastic components.
